Hello there from Five Lab 😎

    Phones endpoints:
  • /phones - to get all phones
  • /phones?page=1&perPage=10&query=text - to get phones by parts
  • /phones/:phoneId - to get one phone
    Phone details endpoints:
  • /phoneInfo/:phoneId - to get full information of the phone
    Tablets endpoints:
  • /tablets - to get all tablets
  • /tablets?page=1&perPage=10&query=text - to get tablets by parts
  • /tablets/:tabletId - to get one tablet
    Categories endpoints:
  • /categories - to get list of categories
